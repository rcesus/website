Look, I know everyone has opinions on this, but mine are correct, so let's get into it.

## The Tiers

**Untouchable:** *Enema of the State* and *Take Off Your Pants and Jacket*. This is the run. This is the stuff that rewired my brain in middle school. You can't convince me otherwise.

**Severely Underrated:** the untitled album (2003). Everyone slept on it because it got weird, and "weird Blink" is the best Blink. "I Miss You" alone earns it a spot near the top.

**Good But We Don't Talk About It:** *Neighborhoods* and *California*. Solid records. Not the soundtrack to my formative years, but they hold up.

**Where It Started:** *Cheshire Cat* and *Dude Ranch*. Foundational. "Dammit" is a perfect song and I won't be taking questions.
